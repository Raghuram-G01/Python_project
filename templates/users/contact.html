Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation and fill out the registration form. You'll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

Can I edit my posts after publishing?

Yes! You can edit your posts at any time by going to your profile and clicking the edit button on any of your published posts.

How do I add images to my posts?

Our rich text editor supports image uploads. Simply click the image button in the editor toolbar and upload your image.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, we offer a fully responsive web experience that works great on mobile devices. A dedicated mobile app is in development.
